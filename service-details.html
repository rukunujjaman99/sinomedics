Neurology

About This Service

Our Neurology department specializes in diagnosing and treating disorders of the brain, spinal cord and nervous system. From migraines and epilepsy to stroke and degenerative conditions, our neurologists provide evidence-based care using advanced diagnostic technology.

Procedures & Treatments

1
EEG (Electroencephalography)

Records brain electrical activity to diagnose seizure disorders.

2
EMG / Nerve Conduction Study

Evaluates muscle and nerve health.

3
MRI Brain & Spine

High-resolution imaging for brain and spinal abnormalities.

4
Stroke Thrombolysis

Emergency treatment for acute stroke patients.

5
Deep Brain Stimulation

Surgical treatment for Parkinson’s disease.
