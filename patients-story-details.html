Age 45 MediCare Central Hospital January 2024
Heart Valve Replacement

My Journey

For years, I lived with a constant feeling of exhaustion. Simple tasks like climbing stairs would leave me breathless. When I was finally diagnosed with severe mitral valve regurgitation, I was terrified.

The surgery was a success. The team monitored me around the clock during recovery. Within weeks, I noticed a dramatic change — I could breathe freely and walk without stopping.

Six months post-surgery, I ran my first 5K. Something I never imagined possible.
